About

A runner at heart

Leo Reeve is a competitive middle distance and cross country runner with a passion for racing that goes beyond the clock. Whether it's the controlled burn of an 800m or the mud and hills of a cross country course, Leo brings the same focus and commitment to every race.

A classified T37 para athlete from Victoria, Leo holds four Australian records across the 800m and 1500m in his class, at both Under 17 and Under 20 level. He continues to train and compete with a drive to keep improving season on season.
